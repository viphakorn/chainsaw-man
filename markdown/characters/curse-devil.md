## Appearance\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fchainsaw-man.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FCurse_Devil%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en "Sign in to edit")\]

When summoned, the Curse Devil manifests as a large, two-headed skeleton with horns, its right skull appears human while the left skull has three eye slits and longer, mandible-like teeth. The Curse Devil has clawed hands and a large distended ribcage.[\[1\]](#cite_note-Ch24Pg10-1)

Whenever it speaks to someone the Curse Devil appears as a dark pair of lips hovering in darkness or as a monstrously large mouth.[\[2\]](#cite_note-Ch24Pg4_-_9-2)[\[3\]](#cite_note-Ch29Pg5_-_6-3) In [episode 10](/wiki/Episode_10 "Episode 10") of the anime this is changed, with the Curse Devil physically appearing behind the one he speaks to.

[![Curse Devil helping Aki fire his nail sword](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/chainsaw-man/images/3/3c/Curse_Devil_helping_Aki_fire_his_nail_sword.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/220?cb=20221129174739)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/chainsaw-man/images/3/3c/Curse_Devil_helping_Aki_fire_his_nail_sword.png/revision/latest?cb=20221129174739)

Curse Devil helping Aki fire his nail sword

During his battle against the [Katana Man](/wiki/Katana_Man "Katana Man"), Aki pulled out his Curse Spike to pierce Katana Man's body three times. This trigger served to summon the Curse Devil on Aki's behalf who manifested behind the Katana Man and picked him up before biting him and killing him.[\[5\]](#cite_note-Ch24Pg1_-_11-5)

Aki later asks the Curse Devil how much life span he has left after using the Curse Devil's power, and the Curse Devil states that he has two years remaining.[\[3\]](#cite_note-Ch29Pg5_-_6-3)

### [International Assassins arc](/wiki/International_Assassins_arc "International Assassins arc")\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fchainsaw-man.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FCurse_Devil%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D6&uselang=en "Sign in to edit")\]

[Tolka](/wiki/Tolka "Tolka")'s master uses a nail to stab [Denji](/wiki/Denji "Denji") three times without him noticing. She explains her contract with the Curse Devil to her apprentice and entrusts the fourth and final stab for him to perform as a test. If he succeeds then she will reveal her other devil contracts to him.[\[6\]](#cite_note-Ch56Pg6_-_7-6)

Tolka lays a trap and manages to stab Denji by having him step on the nail. The Curse Devil appears and picks up Denji, violently biting into him and killing him.[\[7\]](#cite_note-Ch62Pg16_-_19-7)

## Abilities\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fchainsaw-man.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FCurse_Devil%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D7&uselang=en "Sign in to edit")\]

As a Devil, the Curse Devil possesses all of the standard [devil abilities](/wiki/Devil#General_Abilities "Devil"). As a contract devil, it showed its abilities based on its contracts.

**Curse**: Upon it's contractor stabbing their target a few times with its nail, the Curse Devil will manifest itself, pick up the target by their arms in a cross position, and mortally wound them by biting into their shoulders and necks while crushing their arms. The contracts with it seen so far are:

-   In exchange for some of his lifespan, [Aki Hayakawa](/wiki/Aki_Hayakawa "Aki Hayakawa") can curse his targets by stabbing them with his nail-shaped sword 3 times. Each time he stabs his target, he shouts **Fire!** and a finger of the Curse Devil manifests to flick the nail.

-   In exchange for the loss of sensations on several of her fingers, [Santa Claus](/wiki/Santa_Claus "Santa Claus") can curse her targets by stabbing them with a nail 4 times.

As revealed by Santa Claus, the stabbing can also be done by another person, and not necessarily by the contractor himself. In fact, her contract was activated even if it was [Tolka](/wiki/Tolka "Tolka") who stabbed Denji instead of her.

**Invisibility**: When the Curse Devil appears, he appears to have the power to make himself invisible and not be seen by other eyes. Indeed, when Denji was killed by the Curse Devil, neither he nor [Beam](/wiki/Beam "Beam") could see the devil, and it looked as if Denji being crucified while hovering in the air.

## Trivia\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fchainsaw-man.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FCurse_Devil%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D10&uselang=en "Sign in to edit")\]

-   The Curse Devil's rankings in the [Popularity Polls](/wiki/Popularity_Polls "Popularity Polls") are as follows:
    -   The Curse Devil ranked 43rd in the First Popularity Poll with 178 votes.
    -   The Curse Devil and [Taiyo Hayakawa](/wiki/Taiyo_Hayakawa "Taiyo Hayakawa") ranked 62nd in the Second Popularity Poll with 1,279 votes.
-   The Curse Devil's use of rituals involving nails is likely a reference to [Ushi-no-Toki-Mairi](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ushi_no_toki_mairi "wikipedia:Ushi no toki mairi"), a Japanese cursing ritual involving the insertion of a nail into a sacred tree or effigy.
