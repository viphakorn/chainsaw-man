## Plot

### History

At some point [Joey](/joey "Joey"), [Aldo](/aldo "Aldo"), and the unnamed brother made a contract with the Skin Devil.[\[1\]](#cite_note-Ch57Pg13-1)

## Abilities

As a Devil, the Skin Devil possesses all of the standard [devil abilities](/devil#general-abilities "Devil") which includes bestowing contracts upon humans for a price. The contracts with it seen so far are with [Joey](/joey "Joey"), [Aldo](/aldo "Aldo"), and their unnamed brother for an unknown price. Upon touching a corpse, they can change their appearance into that of the corpse.[\[1\]](#cite_note-Ch57Pg13-1)
