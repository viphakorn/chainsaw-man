## Plot

### History

The Four Horsemen and the [Weapon Devils](/weapon-devils "Weapon Devils") once engaged in a brutal battle against [Pochita](/pochita "Pochita") in [Hell](/hell "Hell"). However, in the midst of the battle, Pochita mysteriously vanished. Despite searches by the [Control Devil](/control-devil "Control Devil"), Pochita could not be found. Unbeknownst to the Four Horsemen, this was because Pochita had changed forms on the verge of death and escaped to Earth.

### [Dating Denji arc](/dating-denji-arc "Dating Denji arc")

[Hirofumi Yoshida](/hirofumi-yoshida "Hirofumi Yoshida") knew of the Death Devil's existence, and how by swallowing Devils [Pochita](/pochita "Pochita") could erase their presence from the world. While trapped in the aquarium, he wondered to [Denji](/denji "Denji") if Chainsaw Man would be willing to eat the Death Devil for humanity.
