## Personality\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fchainsaw-man.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FMasaki_Ando%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en "Sign in to edit")\]
