## Appearance

Tendo is a tall woman with black hair that is tied back in a messy ponytail with long side bangs reaching past her shoulders.

She tends to wear the standard Public Safety Devil Extermination uniform, and has a horizontal scar across her face—a feature she shares with [Yutaro Kurose](/wiki/Yutaro_Kurose "Yutaro Kurose").[\[1\]](#cite_note-Ch26Pg16-1)

## Personality

Like Yutaro, Michiko is nervous around Makima and insists that she doesn't want to join the special division.[\[2\]](#cite_note-Ch29Pg19-2) Michiko appears to have some sensitivity as she suggested stepping out to give Aki some space when she and Yutaro found him crying.[\[3\]](#cite_note-Ch29Pg14-3)

## Plot

### History

Michiko's life was ruined by the [Gun Devil](/wiki/Gun_Devil "Gun Devil") which was her motivation for joining Public Safety though she never tried to kill it herself, believing it to be impossible.[\[4\]](#cite_note-Ch33Pg4-4)

### [Katana Man arc](/wiki/Katana_Man_arc "Katana Man arc")

Michiko and Yutaro were waiting at a train station in Kyoto for [Makima](/wiki/Makima "Makima")'s arrival when they received the word that Makima had been killed on the train in an ambush. Makima disembarks the train covered in blood but unharmed. When the two of them ask her if she was shot, Makima simply responds that she wasn't.[\[5\]](#cite_note-Ch26Pg16_-_19-5)

Makima tells Michiko to rent out a nearby shrine with the highest possible altitude so that they can assist the special divisions in Tokyo.[\[6\]](#cite_note-Ch27Pg1_-_2-6) They travel to a nearby shrine and are both blindfolded while Makima carries out her attack on the terrorists; Yutaro wonders why they need to be blindfolded and Tendo tells him that ordinary devil hunters aren't authorized to know which devils she is contracted with. Once Makima finished, she tells them to remove their blindfolds and that she would be returning to Tokyo.[\[7\]](#cite_note-Ch27Pg10_-_19-7)

As they arrive in Tokyo, they are met by [Madoka](/wiki/Madoka "Madoka"), who passes on a message to Makima and resigns. Nervously, Michiko and Yutaro look at each other and remind Makima that they don't want to join the special division and that they'll only be around for a week to help with training.[\[8\]](#cite_note-Ch28Pg14_-_19-8)

Michiko and Yutaro travel to the hospital to meet Aki, who is recovering after his battle with the [Katana Man](/wiki/Katana_Man "Katana Man"). They introduced themselves and stated they were there by Makima's request to train the Special Division 4.[\[3\]](#cite_note-Ch29Pg14-3)

Michiko expresses surprise that Aki decided to stay in the Special Division, informing him if he wanted to still be of use, he would have to make a contract with a stronger devil. After Aki shows his determination, she leaves with Kurose and they state that they would be back the next day with the necessary paperwork.[\[9\]](#cite_note-Ch30Pg10_-_13-9)

Michiko and Yutaro lead Aki to an underground facility where devils are kept by Public Safety. When Kurose asks Aki if the woman who visited him at the hospital was his girlfriend, Tendo told him to get his mind out of the gutter.[\[10\]](#cite_note-Ch31Pg12_-_13-10) They arrive at the cell containing the Future Devil, which has contracts with two other Public Safety devil hunters.[\[11\]](#cite_note-Ch31Pg17-11)

Michiko and Yutaro drop Aki off by car once he's made his deal with the Future Devil, and plan to go sightseeing before returning to Kyoto.[\[12\]](#cite_note-Ch33Pg1_-_7-12)

### [International Assassins arc](/wiki/International_Assassins_arc "International Assassins arc")

Yutaro, Michiko and [Subaru](/wiki/Subaru "Subaru"), a devil hunter from Kyoto, were summoned to Tokyo to be additional guards for [Denji](/wiki/Denji "Denji").[\[13\]](#cite_note-Ch55Pg4-13) In the car ride there, Subaru asks them about Makima since they've both met her before but, before Michiko can tell Subaru anything, a set of road spikes are thrown in front of the car, causing them to crash. Three American devil hunters break the car windows and gun them down, killing them in the process. [Joey](/wiki/Joey "Joey") comments it was a shame they had to kill a woman.[\[14\]](#cite_note-Ch55Pg7_-_14-14)

### [Gun Devil arc](/wiki/Gun_Devil_arc "Gun Devil arc")

On the beach, Michiko's body is seen chained to Makima, along with Yutaro, Princi, Aki, the Angel Devil, and Sawatari. Tendo and Kurose's contract with the Punishment Devil is used along with the other devil powers against the Gun Devil.

## Abilities

### Devil Contracts

Tendo had a contract with the [Punishment Devil](/wiki/Punishment_Devil "Punishment Devil") for an unknown price.

## Etymology

-   The name **[Michiko](https://www.behindthename.com/name/michiko)** is written in katakana (ミチコ), which has no semantic meaning. However, when written in kanji (美智子), it could possibly mean "beautiful" (<ruby lang="ja"><rb>美</rb><rp> (</rp><rt>み</rt><rp>) </rp></ruby> , _**mi**_[?](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets "wikipedia:Help:Installing Japanese character sets")), "wisdom, intellect" (<ruby lang="ja"><rb>智</rb><rp> (</rp><rt>ち</rt><rp>) </rp></ruby> , _**chi**_[?](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets "wikipedia:Help:Installing Japanese character sets")) and "child" (<ruby lang="ja"><rb>子</rb><rp> (</rp><rt>こ</rt><rp>) </rp></ruby> , _**ko**_[?](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:Installing_Japanese_character_sets "wikipedia:Help:Installing Japanese character sets")).
-   Michiko's surname **Tendo** means "cherub, gods disguised as children, children parading as cherubs" (<ruby lang="ja"><rb>天</rb><rp> (</rp><rt>てん</rt><rp>) </rp></ruby> <ruby lang="ja"><rb>童</rb><rp> (</rp><rt>どう</rt><rp>) </rp></ruby> ).
