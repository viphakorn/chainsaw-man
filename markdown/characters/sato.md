## Plot\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fchainsaw-man.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FSato%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D3&uselang=en "Sign in to edit")\]

### [International Assassins arc](/wiki/International_Assassins_arc "International Assassins arc")\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fchainsaw-man.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FSato%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D4&uselang=en "Sign in to edit")\]

When [Santa Claus](/wiki/Santa_Claus "Santa Claus") attack's Denji's group with his dolls, [Nakamura](/wiki/Nakamura "Nakamura") calls the Public Safety HQ for backup, with Sato and several others arriving to help. Shortly after arriving, he is quickly decapitated by [Quanxi](/wiki/Quanxi "Quanxi") in her massacre along with the others as they faced Quanxi's harem.
